## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Click "Register" in the navigation menu
2. Choose your account type:
   - **Patient**: For individuals managing their diabetes
   - **Companion**: For caregivers, family members, or healthcare providers
3. Fill in your details:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password
4. Click "Sign Up"

### Login
1. Click "Login" in the navigation menu
2. Enter your email and password
3. Select your account type (Patient or Companion)
4. Click "Sign In"

## Patient Features

### Medication Management
1. Click "Medications" in the navigation menu
2. To add a new medication:
   - Click "Add Medication"
   - Enter medication details:
     - Name
     - Dosage
     - Frequency (Once daily, Twice daily, etc.)
     - Time
   - Click "Save Medication"
3. To mark medications as taken:
   - Find the medication in your list
   - Click "Mark as Taken" to log completion
4. To edit medications:
   - Click "Edit" on any medication entry
   - Update the details as needed
   - Click "Save Changes"

### Health Logging

#### Glucose Tracking
1. Click "Health Logger" then "Glucose Records"
2. To add a new reading:
   - Enter your glucose level (mg/dL)
   - Select date and time
   - Click "Save Record"
3. View your glucose history in the table below

#### Blood Pressure Logging
1. Click "Health Logger" then "Blood Pressure Records"
2. To add a new reading:
   - Enter systolic pressure (mm Hg)
   - Enter diastolic pressure (mm Hg)
   - Select date and time
   - Click "Save Record"
3. View your blood pressure history in the table below

### Health Reports
1. Click "Reports" in the navigation menu
2. Choose your export format:
   - PDF: Comprehensive formatted report
   - CSV: Spreadsheet-compatible data
3. Click "Download" to save your report

### Managing Companions

#### Reviewing Access Requests
1. Click "Connections" in the navigation menu
2. Under "Pending Requests":
   - Review companion details
   - Click "Accept" or "Decline"

#### Setting Access Levels
1. For accepted companions, set access levels:
   - Medication Records:
     - No Access: Cannot view
     - View Only: Can see records
     - Full Access: Can view and edit
   - Glucose Records:
     - No Access/View Only/Full Access
   - Blood Pressure Records:
     - No Access/View Only/Full Access
2. Click "Update Access" to save changes

## Companion Features

### Linking with Patients
1. After registering as a companion:
   - You'll be prompted to enter a patient's email
   - The patient must approve your request
2. Wait for patient approval

### Viewing Patient Data
1. After approval, view granted data:
   - Medications (if access granted)
   - Glucose records (if access granted)
   - Blood pressure records (if access granted)
2. If you have edit access:
   - Can update medication logs
   - Can add/edit health records
3. If export permission granted:
   - Can download PDF/CSV reports

## Notes
- Companions can only access data specifically shared by patients
- Patients can modify or revoke companion access at any time
- Data modifications by companions are logged in the system

## Value Ranges and Restrictions
- **Glucose Levels**: 70-180 mg/dL
- **Blood Pressure**:
  - Systolic: 90-180 mm Hg
  - Diastolic: 60-120 mm Hg
- **Medication Frequency Options**:
  - Once Daily
  - Twice Daily
  - Three Times Daily
